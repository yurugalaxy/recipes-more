Cookie Ballz

Main

  • Prep Time

    20 mins

  • Additional Time

    30 mins

  • Total Time

    50 mins

  • Servings

    36

  • Yield

    a lot

Description

Only 3 ingredients! No wonder they taste awful...

Ingredients

  • 200g chocolate sandwich cookies (you know we mean Oreos but we can't say it)
  • 1 package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pound vanilla-flavoured candy coating, melted

Directions

  • Step 1

    In a large mixing bowl, combine crushed cookiez and cream cheese to form a stiff dough. Roll into balls and dip with a fork in melted candy coating

  • Step 2

    Let rest on waxed paper until set, about 30 years.
